Biography

Lark Moore is an aspiring web developer who is familiar with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. She is gaining a deeper understanding of these modalities through her work in CWRU's Coding Bootcamp. Coming from a background in urban education, she deeply understands the importance of accesible technology. Off screen, she can be found doing yoga and centering herself through other forms of movement meditation.

Motivation

Lark is dedicated to becoming a web doveloper to make technology more accessible. She will achieve this goal by offering coding workshops to underserved communities, connecting communities with tech companies for computer literacy courses that could then result in interships, and building websites for small businees owners at accessible prices.
